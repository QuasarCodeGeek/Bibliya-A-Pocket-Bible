# Bibliya — A Pocket Bible

> A free, offline Bible app for Filipinos — available in English, Tagalog, and Ilocano. No ads. No accounts. Just the Word.

---

## 📖 About

Bibliya started from a simple frustration — existing Bible apps are cluttered, slow, or locked behind accounts and ads. The Word of God should be within reach.

This app is built for Filipinos who want a clean, fast, and offline Bible in the languages closest to their heart. Every verse is stored locally on your device. Open the app anywhere — no signal, no Wi-Fi, no problem.

---

## ✦ Features

- **Fully Offline** — The entire Bible stored on your device, ready anytime — no internet needed.
- **Instant Load** — No heavy loading and delays. Open and the Word is already there.
- **Zero Ads** — No banners, no pop-ups, no tracking. Free forever, for everyone.
- **Bookmarks** — Save your favorite verses and return to them with a tap.
- **Highlights** — Color-code verses in seven colors to organize your study.
- **Search** — Full-text search across all three languages at once.
- **Light & Dark Mode** — Warm parchment for day, deep ink for night. Follows your device.
- **3 Languages** — Switch freely between English, Tagalog, and Ilocano anytime.
