# Bibliya — A Pocket Bible

> A free, offline Bible app for Filipinos — available in English, Tagalog, and Ilocano. No ads. No accounts. Just the Word.

---

## 📖 About

Bibliya is a simple, offline Bible app built with one goal in mind: making God's Word accessible anytime, anywhere.

Many Bible apps today require an internet connection, include advertisements, or ask users to create accounts. Bibliya removes those barriers by keeping every verse stored locally on your device.

Whether you're at home, attending church, traveling, or living in areas with limited internet access, Scripture is always within reach.

---

## ✨ Features

- **📵 Fully Offline** — Read the Bible anytime without an internet connection.
- **⚡ Instant Load** — Open the app and start reading immediately.
- **🚫 Zero Ads** — No advertisements, pop-ups, or tracking.
- **🔖 Bookmarks** — Save your favorite verses for quick access.
- **🖊 Highlights** — Organize your study with seven highlight colors.
- **🔍 Full-Text Search** — Search across English, Tagalog, and Ilocano translations.
- **🌙 Light & Dark Mode** — Automatically follows your device theme.
- **🌐 Three Languages** — Easily switch between English, Tagalog, and Ilocano.
